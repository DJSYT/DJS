Editing Process

My Workflow

Creating each edit involves several steps:

  • Scene Selection: Carefully choosing the most impactful moments from the anime
  • Music Selection: Finding the perfect track that matches the emotion and pacing
  • Editing: Using CapCut, Alight Motion, and other tools for cutting and effects
  • Color Grading: Enhancing the visual appeal with proper color correction
  • Final Touch: Adding transitions, text animations, and final polish

Tools I Use

  • CapCut: Primary editing software for cutting and basic effects
  • Alight Motion: Advanced motion graphics and complex effects
  • VN: Quick edits and mobile editing
  • Prisma3D: 3D text animations and dimensional effects
