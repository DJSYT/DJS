Common Questions

Here are answers to the most frequently asked questions about my content and channels:

What kind of projects do you work on?

I focus primarily on three areas: video editing (especially anime AMVs), programming (web development and small applications), and animations using Flipaclip. You can find examples across my YouTube channels!

Where can I follow your content?

I have three YouTube channels: DJS_YT for gaming content, DJS_Vortex for anime edits and AMVs, and DJS_ANIMATION for my original animations. All links are available on the home page!

Do you take project requests?

Currently, I don't take on commissioned projects since I'm still young and focusing on learning. However, I'm always open to collaboration ideas and feedback! Feel free to reach out with suggestions.

How often do you post new content?

I try to maintain a consistent schedule and post new content every couple of weeks. The frequency depends on my school schedule and the complexity of the projects I'm working on.

What tools do you use for animations?

For animations, I primarily use Flipaclip, which is perfect for creating smooth 2D animations on mobile devices. It's user-friendly and has all the features I need for my creative projects.

What editing software do you use?

I use a variety of tools depending on the project: CapCut for general video editing, Alight Motion for advanced effects, VN for quick edits, and Prisma3D for 3D text animations and effects.

Which games do you play?

I mainly play Minecraft and Roblox. These are the games I'm most passionate about and feature regularly on my DJS_YT channel. I enjoy both creative building and adventure gameplay!

Can I collaborate with you?

I'm definitely open to collaborations! If you have an interesting project idea that aligns with my interests in gaming, editing, or animation, feel free to reach out through my social media channels.

How did you get started with content creation?

I started as a hobby, experimenting with different creative outlets. What began as simple gaming recordings evolved into more complex editing projects and eventually animation work. I'm still learning and improving every day!

What's your favorite type of content to create?

While I enjoy all my creative work, I have a special passion for anime edits and AMVs. There's something magical about combining great music with stunning anime visuals to create an emotional experience.

Still Have Questions?

  • Contact me on Discord
